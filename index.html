AMD Vitis AI Documentation

IP Discontinuation Notice

AMD Vitis AI 5.0, and prior releases, are based on the Deep Learning Processing Unit (DPU) IP technology which is now discontinued.

AMD Vitis AI now uses the new Neural Processing Unit (NPU) IP technology and is General Access for:

  • Versal AI Edge Series
  • Versal AI Edge Series Gen 2

Please visit the Vitis AI Developer Hub or consult the Vitis AI Documentation for installation instructions & more details.

For Vitis AI support of AMD Versal™ AI Core and Zynq™ UltraScale+™ MPSoC with the NPU technology, or any roadmap related inquiries please contact your AMD sales representative.
